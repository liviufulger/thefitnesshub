15 Minute chicken meal preparation

Healthy Food Recipe

This 15-minute meal prep recipe is simple, high in protein, and can be cooked in 15 minutes.

To make this oven baked chicken and vegetable meal prep you will need:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 bags microwavable brown rice
  • 2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 courgette
  • 2 tablespoon oregano
  • 2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 red peppers
  • 1 red onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Place flattened chicken breasts onto a baking sheet after butterflying.
  3. Add the red onion, red pepper, and courgette to the tray with the chicken, diced into chunks.
  4. Drizzle over olive oil on all the ingredients.
  5. Mix oregano, garlic powder, and onion powder with salt and pepper, then sprinkle over the mixture.
  6. Pop into oven for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Make up the box with half a bag of rice and a handful of spinach.

 By preparing healthy meals in advance, you can ensure that you have a dinner ready to go, save time, and save money.
