MENTAL STRATEGIES TO IMPROVE YOUR WORKOUTS

Intelectual Thinking Strong Mind

When it comes to bodybuilding issues, most of us turn to outside help. Have you considered that perhaps the real secret to improvement lies outside as well as inside the gym? A computer that is more powerful than any that has ever been created is implanted between your ears, not on your desk. This refers to both your thoughts and your brain.

The best mindset strategies for achieving your fitness goals:

Visualize Succes

Think about your ideal appearance every night before you go to bed. Create a mental picture of every muscle in your body, and then visualize your workout for the next day from beginning to end.

Be Positive

Approach every workout with enthusiasm and positivity. Your training today should be more productive than yesterday. It will feel easier to lift the weights, and each exercise will feel lighter.

Be Aggressive

Make every rep count. Don't let anything stop you from lifting like you're on the line. Put all your anger and emotions into every rep of every set.

Focus

Keep your focus during a set. During rest, think about what you will do next. Drop the newspaper, put aside the phone, and stop chit-chatting. Maintain a laser-like focus on the body area you're working on and create a "mind/muscle" connection.

Always keep in mind that your time spent working out is a gift. Don't let a second go to waste.
